**For Body Composition (Pull-ups + Vanity Goals):**

You mentioned "love handles, man boobs, doughy chest." Strength alone won't fix this - need slight caloric deficit.

**Simple Framework:**
- Protein: 150-180g/day (0.8-1g per lb bodyweight)
